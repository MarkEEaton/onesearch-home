Here's what you will and won't find with OneSearch:

Books at Kingsborough
By default OneSearch searches only the Kingsborough library, but you can expand to other CUNY libraries by using the dropdown menu on the right-hand side of the search bar.
Ebooks
Including online government publications
DVDs
Articles
Many—but not all—journal, magazine and newspaper articles are available through OneSearch. You must sign in to see the most results.
Textbooks
Many textbooks be be found through OneSearch, but certain textbooks can only be found through EReserves
Books at other libraries
You can search thousands of libraries nationwide through Worldcat, the world's largest network of library content and services.
Entries from reference books
You can find reference books listed on OneSearch, but not individual entries. Check out Gale eBooks for encyclopedia entries!
